To deep clean your ceiling fan, start by turning off the power to the fan and placing a drop cloth underneath to catch any falling debris. Use a damp microfiber cloth or a vacuum attachment to carefully wipe down each blade, being sure to remove all dust buildup.

Over time, crumbs, grease, and other debris can collect in these spaces, attracting pests and creating unpleasant odors.
